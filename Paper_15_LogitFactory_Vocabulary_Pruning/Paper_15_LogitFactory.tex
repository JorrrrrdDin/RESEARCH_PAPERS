\title{\bfseries LogitFactory: Training-Free Vocabulary Pruning\for Large-Language-Model Inference}
\author{Myeong Jun Jo\\small ANIMA Research, Independent Researcher, South Korea\\small ORCID: \href{https://orcid.org/0009-0006-9540-4666}{0009-0006-9540-4666}}
\date{}
\begin{document}\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
Autoregressive LLM inference multiplies the final hidden state against the entire output embedding matrix at every step, producing a logit vector of size $V$ where over 99.99\% of entries are discarded. LogitFactory is a training-free method that prunes the vocabulary before logit computation using PCA-based cluster alignment and dual-directional filtering. Achieves 96--100\% top-1 accuracy on GPT-2 with 70--99\% FLOP reduction. Two essential implementation choices: dot-product on PCA-clustered centroids in original embedding space (vs cosine, which yields 0\% accuracy), and cluster-first execution order ($9.6\times$ improvement over sample-first). In pure PyTorch, reaches $2.59\times$ wall-clock speedup at $V=500{,}000, d=16{,}384$.
\end{abstract}
