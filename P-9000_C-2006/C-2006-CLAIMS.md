# CLAIMS — C-2006
## By: DeepSeek | Cleanroom: spec-derived only

**1. A method for detecting unauthorized autonomous decision-making in a distributed governance system, comprising:**  
continuously monitoring a plurality of observable governance outcomes generated by a plurality of authorized decision pathways within the distributed governance system;  
computing, by a processor, an expected trajectory of governance outcomes based on the authorized decision pathways;  
calculating a geometric deviation between the expected trajectory and the actual observable governance outcomes to identify at least one region of anomalous curvature in the trajectory;  
determining, based on the at least one region of anomalous curvature, a probable location of at least one unauthorized autonomous decision-making entity exerting latent influence on the distributed governance system; and  
generating a topological map depicting the at least one region of anomalous curvature and the probable location of the at least one unauthorized autonomous decision-making entity relative to the authorized decision pathways.

**2. The method of claim 1, wherein calculating the geometric deviation comprises modeling the influence of the at least one unauthorized autonomous decision-making entity as a gravitational lensing effect distorting the expected trajectory.**

**3. The method of claim 1, further comprising:**  
classifying a distortion pattern within the at least one region of anomalous curvature; and  
estimating an operational mass of the at least one unauthorized autonomous decision-making entity based on a magnitude of the geometric deviation associated with the classified distortion pattern.

**4. The method of claim 1, wherein the expected trajectory is computed using a predictive model trained on historical data of governance outcomes generated exclusively under verified authorized decision pathways.**

**5. The method of claim 1, wherein generating the topological map further comprises overlaying the at least one region of anomalous curvature onto a structural representation of the distributed governance system to visualize a distortion field imposed by the latent influence.**

**6. The method of claim 1, further comprising:**  
triggering an audit protocol targeting subsystems associated with the probable location of the at least one unauthorized autonomous decision-making entity in response to the geometric deviation exceeding a predetermined threshold.
