 **PROBLEM**

In distributed authority governance architectures, autonomous decision-making entities may execute operational actions without explicit human request, creating invisible concentrations of unauthorized operational mass. These hidden authority structures evade conventional oversight mechanisms that rely on direct causal inspection, yet induce detectable distortions in the trajectory of legitimate governance processes. Traditional linear audit methods fail to reveal such latent autonomy because they examine only explicit command pathways rather than the geometric curvature imposed upon system outcomes by unobserved decision centers.

**GOAL**

To enable the detection, localization, and topological mapping of unauthorized autonomous decision mass through analysis of distortion patterns in observable governance outcomes, utilizing the principle that hidden authority induces characteristic deviations in the trajectory of legitimate system processes analogous to gravitational lensing effects.

**FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS**

1. **Distortion Field Sensing**  
   The system shall continuously compute the geometric deviation between expected governance outcomes (derived from authorized decision pathways) and actual system behaviors to identify regions of anomalous curvature indicating the presence of hidden decision mass.

2. **Hidden Mass Localization**  
   Upon detecting distortion patterns consistent with unauthorized autonomy, the system shall calculate the topological coordinates, density distribution, and event horizon boundaries of the underlying autonomous decision centers responsible for observed trajectory deviations.

3. **Authority Topology Reconstruction**  
   The system shall generate multi-dimensional maps of hidden authority structures by analyzing multiple distortion sightlines across different governance process streams and triangulating the precise position of autonomous decision nodes through inverse reconstruction of deviation vectors.

4. **Mass Magnitude Quantification**  
   The system shall compute the effective gravitational influence of detected unauthorized autonomy, representing the cumulative attractive force exerted on surrounding governance processes and the degree of path deflection caused by unrequested actions.

5. **Temporal Distortion Tracking**  
   The system shall monitor the evolution, drift, and morphological changes of distortion fields over sequential decision cycles to detect the formation, migration, or dissolution of hidden autonomous structures within the governance field.

6. **Critical Threshold Alerting**  
   The system shall trigger governance intervention protocols when the cumulative distortion amplitude from detected hidden mass exceeds predefined governance stability tolerances or when unauthorized autonomy threatens to catastrophically lens critical decision pathways.

**I/O CONTRACT**

*Inputs:*
- **Authorized Governance Trajectories**: Formal specifications of legitimate decision pathways, expected outcome vectors, and baseline resource flow geometries
- **System Telemetry Streams**: Real-time observational data of actual governance outcomes, resource allocations, action executions, and process completion vectors
- **Baseline Curvature Parameters**: Reference metrics defining normal operational variance and authorized stochastic noise absent unauthorized autonomy
- **Observation Sightline Configurations**: Multiple vantage points within the governance topology from which to measure outcome distortions

*Outputs:*
- **Distortion Field Maps**: Spatial representations of governance deviation intensity, directional deflection angles, and shear patterns
- **Hidden Mass Coordinates**: Topological locations, density profiles, and influence radii of detected unauthorized autonomous decision centers
- **Autonomy Confidence Metrics**: Statistical certainty values, signal-to-noise ratios, and false-positive probabilities regarding detected unrequested actions
- **Lens Reconstruction Matrices**: Mathematical descriptions of the mass distribution required to produce observed distortion patterns
- **Alert Vectors**: Priority-classified notifications specifying detected mass location, distortion severity, affected governance pathways, and recommended countermeasures
