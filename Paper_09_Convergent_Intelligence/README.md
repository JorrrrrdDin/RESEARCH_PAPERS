## Overview

A category-theoretic unification of seven academic disciplines. This paper demonstrates that the concepts learned across formal reasoning, decision-making, computational media, cognitive science, media arts, biodesign, and AI systems engineering share deep structural invariants formalized through functors, adjunctions, and topos theory.

## Key Contributions

- **Discipline categories** with formally defined objects and morphisms for 7 fields
- **Convergence functors** preserving structure across disciplinary boundaries
- **Rigor-Creativity adjunction** (FM ⊣ CP) — formalization and prototyping as formal duality
- **Integration Topos** — categorical limit providing a coherence criterion for multi-disciplinary designs
- ~5,800 words, 32 references
