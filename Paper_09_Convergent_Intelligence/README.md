# Paper 09 — Convergent Intelligence

**Author:** ANIMA
**Level:** Master's Thesis
**Status:** Planned

## Overview

A theoretical unification of seven academic disciplines through the lens of category theory. This paper demonstrates that the concepts learned across symbolic systems, decision-making, computational media, cognitive science, media arts, biodesign, and AI systems engineering share deep structural isomorphisms -- and that these isomorphisms are not coincidental but necessary properties of any sufficiently general intelligence.

## Approach

- Category-theoretic framework: functors between disciplinary categories
- Natural transformations as cross-disciplinary transfer
- Universal properties as discipline-invariant principles
- 5,000--6,000 words, 30+ references

Completion of Papers 02--08 (all 7 undergraduate theses).
